Mochipanko

I´m Liya aka mochipanko and a freelance illustrator, designer and tattoo artist based in Hamburg, Germany. Even though I´ve graduated in Interior Architecture, I´ve decided to go with my real passion for my career - which is drawing. One of my favorite themes is nature, I love studying the anatomy of animals, looking at different types of flora and incorporating them in my work whenever I can. When people tell me they feel comfortable and cozy looking at my art, I´m happy!
