Product description

The shikishi art book by Heikala is a collection of original paintings and drawings made on shikishi boards between 2019 and 2021. The 45 artworks featured in this book were created on shikishi boards measuring 120 x 135 millimeters or 4,7 x 5,3 inches in size, with varying paper materials. Several different mediums were utilized in their creation, including alcohol markers, poster colors, watercolors, and inks
